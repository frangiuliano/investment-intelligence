## Always check

- Risk only capital you can afford to lose; predefine risk/invalidation before
  leaning on a stance.
- Frame outcomes as expectancy over a series of similar setups; audit honestly.
- After the thesis is set, avoid babysitting charts out of discomfort.
- Respect when conditions are dead — do not force noise reads.

## Materiality heuristics

| Signal | Suggested materiality | Notes |
|--------|----------------------|-------|
| Motivational / destiny / promo framing | low | Ignore for alerts |
| Fear/FOMO/revenge with no company event | low | Process smell |
| Predefined risk + invalidation on a thesis | — | Process quality; not a solo materiality upgrade |

## Invalidation

- No risk boundary before the stance.
- Rules changed mid-sample after a few outcomes.
- Fear filters contradictory evidence.
- Micromanaging after commitment.

## Do not

- Map lotaje / MetaTrader / “comprá ahora” into product orders.
- Treat courage/faith slogans as method.
- Equate confidence with certainty.
