## Always check

- Prefer OB claims with HTF premise + ~61–79% pullback + liquidity at prior
  swing + nearby S/R.
- Sketch Buy/Sell model only as narrative (accum → stop-hunt → SMR), not intent.
- Multi-TF: structure first on D1/H4; lower TF only for reaction at predefined zone.
- Fair-value ~50% mainly in ranges; weak in strong trends.
