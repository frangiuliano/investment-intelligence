## Always check

- Prefer stochastic / Bollinger reads in **range** regimes; state trend vs range.
- Divergence and squeezes are cautions / watches — not timed buy/sell certainty.
- Prefer candlestick confluence at band extremes when citing reversals.

## Materiality heuristics

| Signal | Suggested materiality | Notes |
|--------|----------------------|-------|
| Stoch/Bollinger jargon alone | low | Not a catalyst |
| Squeeze + structure + company event | low–medium | Technical lens only |
| Certainty that OB levels must reverse | low | Process smell |

## Invalidation

- Oscillator logic in strong trend without regime note; squeeze treated as directional forecast.

## Do not

- Map 80/20 or outer-band recipes to product orders; invent universal indicator settings.
