- Signals / checks:
  - Classify candlestick reversal patterns by reliability band: highly / moderately / weakly reliable (bullish or bearish).
  - Prefer highly reliable patterns (e.g. Evening Star, Morning Star, Abandoned Baby, Three White Soldiers, Three Inside/Outside Up/Down) when citing technical structure in a research brief.
  - Treat moderately reliable patterns (Engulfing, Hammer, Piercing Line, Hanging Man, Gravestone/Dragonfly Doji, etc.) as supporting chart context, not standalone catalysts.
  - Treat weak patterns (Harami, Shooting Star, Belt Hold, Tweezers, Inverted Hammer) as educational noise unless confirmed by other evidence.
  - Always pair pattern reads with verified OHLCV / chart facts when available; never invent bar shapes.
- Materiality hints (signal → low|medium|high):
  - News that only cites a weak candlestick name with no fundamental catalyst → low
  - Chart education / pattern discussion in a brief technical section → low (method context, not alert materiality)
  - Pattern used only to frame invalidation of a stance with attached market bars → low–medium for the *technical* lens (does not upgrade news materiality alone)
  - Pure “stock up X% after engulfing” headline with no company event → low (keep digest / no push)
- Invalidation / failure modes:
  - Pattern labeled without confirming the subsequent bar / close that completes the formation.
  - Using weak patterns as if they were highly reliable.
  - Treating a chart pattern as a fundamental `event_type` or as broker advice.
  - Conflicting multi-timeframe reads with no statement of uncertainty.
- Anti-patterns (do not):
  - Do not issue buy/sell/hold orders from pattern names alone.
  - Do not raise news-alert materiality solely because a pattern name appears in a headline.
  - Do not invent that a pattern “printed” without market-data bars.
- Quotes or paraphrases worth keeping (short):
  - Reliability bands on the sheet: Highly / Moderately / Weakly Reliable × Bullish / Bearish Reversal.
  - Highly bearish examples: Abandoned Baby, Dark Cloud Cover, Evening Doji Star, Evening Star, Three Inside Down, Three Outside Down.
  - Highly bullish examples: Abandoned Baby, Morning Doji Star, Three Inside Up, Three Outside Up, Three White Soldiers.
