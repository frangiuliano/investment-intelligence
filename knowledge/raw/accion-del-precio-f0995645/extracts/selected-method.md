### Extract: accion-del-precio (Fin-ranked selected chunks)

Ranking: `technical_analysis` via `filter-themes.json` v1.1.0.
Selected 6 / 7 chunks. Spanish price-action / candle deck — paraphrase
**research lens** only; discard binary-options, martingale, pip recipes, and
“trade muy segura” language.

- Signals / checks:
  - Prefer reading structure from **candles alone** (OHLC shapes over time) as
    a visual lens — still require verified bars before asserting a pattern.
  - **Support / resistance as zones** of buyer/seller interest: mark early swing
    rejections; wicks often show the test/pullback at the zone.
  - Break conviction: large bodies / “free fall” into a level, **close beyond**
    the zone with little opposing wick → stronger break narrative; broken
    support often becomes future resistance (and vice versa).
  - Soft tests vs hard breaks: price bouncing in a range until one side breaks
    with decision candles; shrinking candles into a base then decision thrust
    can mark exhaustion → reversal narratives (V-style).
  - Candle families (confirm with prior trend + next action): small body + long
    lower/upper shadow (hammer / shooting-star family); doji / neutral =
    indecision — weak alone without trend; engulfing / covering prior body
    (~50%+ / strong decision covering indecision); three consecutive strong
    bodies with little opposing wick (soldiers/crows family) — larger/separated
    bodies → stronger narrative; min/max of the three as temporary S/R.
  - Decision vs indecision: large decision bodies after pattern cues; oversized
    decision bars often invite a small next-bar correction — do not treat one
    huge bar as certainty.
  - Chart structure: rejection at prior S/R (close fails to pierce; wick shows
    reclaim); triangles = S/R compression until a side breaks (often with
    trendline); avoid leaning near the apex alone; broken channel/triangle
    continues in break direction until structure fails.
  - Classic Fib (23.6–61.8% etc.) as crowd-level expectations inside a swing —
    not standalone.
  - Weinstein-style notes: breakouts with volume / clear thrust matter more than
    soft pierces; MA context for trend regime; “poco” vs “muy” directional when
    MAs and S/R are crossed with force.
  - Indicator confluence only: MACD trend side / mid flip; choppy small MACD
    peaks = messy market → wait; stochastic “clean” waves vs noise; Bollinger
    squeeze (bands tight) → watch reactivation after a band break — bands
    update continuously so treat cautiously; **divergence** (price vs oscillator)
    as correction caution.
  - Macro calendar: high-impact prints can move markets in seconds — note
    timing even for technical theses (do not invent event outcomes).
  - Process: write rules (when enter / when skip / risk %); discipline, patience,
    cold blood; reject emotional “impulse” overrides; no miracle 90% strategy
    claims — expectancy over long samples.
- Materiality hints (signal → low|medium|high):
  - Headline only names price action / engulfing / triangle / Fib → low
  - Pattern jargon as sole upgrade to an alert → low
  - Verified S/R break + volume/structure + company catalyst → low–medium
  - Certainty from one candle or “safe” short-TF recipe → low
- Invalidation / failure modes:
  - Neutral/doji in no-trend treated as a directional signal.
  - Soft wick pierce treated as a confirmed S/R break.
  - Pattern without prior trend or without follow-through after decision bar.
  - Trading the triangle apex / ignoring compression context.
  - Indicator stack in chop (MACD/stoch “revuelto”) forced into a thesis.
  - Martingale / doubling after losses framed as risk management.
- Anti-patterns (do not):
  - Do not map “entrar en cruce estocástico / MM 9×14 / 200 pips / binarias”
    into product orders.
  - Do not promote martingale or binary-options sizing.
  - Do not invent English pattern names when the deck only shows shapes —
    describe OHLC criteria.
  - Do not promote pattern names to `event_type`.
- Quotes or paraphrases worth keeping (short):
  - “Soporte roto, resistencia futura” (and the reverse).
  - Large close beyond support with little wick → break force narrative.
  - Written strategy rules beat divine impulses.
  - Choppy indicator waves → wait for cleaner structure.
