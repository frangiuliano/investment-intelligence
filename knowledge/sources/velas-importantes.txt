- Dark Cloud Cover
- Evening Doji Star
- Evening Star
- Three Inside Down
- Three Outside Down

Moderately Reliable Bearish Reversal Patterns
- Advance Block
- Breakaway Candlestick
- Deliberation
- Downside Tasuki
- Dragonfly Doji (context-dependent when used as bearish)
- Engulfing (bearish)
- Meeting Lines
- Tri Stars
- Gravestone Doji
- Hanging Man
- Meeting Lines Non-Fx

Weak Bearish Reversal Patterns
- Belt Hold (bearish)
- Harami
- Shooting Star
- Harami Cross
- Tweezers Top

Highly Reliable Bullish Reversal Patterns

- Morning Doji Star
- Three Inside Up
- Three Outside Up
- Three White Soldiers

Moderately Reliable Bullish Reversal Patterns
- Breakaway
- Doji Star

- Three Stars In The South

Weak Bullish Reversal Patterns
- Belt Hold (bullish)
- Gravestone Doji (context-dependent)
- Inverted Hammer
- Tweezers Bottom

Method notes implied by the sheet:
- Treat pattern class (highly / moderately / weakly reliable) as a confidence weight for technical reads, not as a trade signal.
- Prefer highly reliable patterns when discussing technical invalidation or stance rationale with chart context.
- Weak patterns alone should not drive high materiality for news alerts; they are chart-education context.
